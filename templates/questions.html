Section 1: REM Sleep Behavior (10 Questions)

Assessing for Rapid Eye Movement Sleep Behavior Disorder (RBD).

1. Do you act out vivid or intense dreams?
2. Have you ever fallen out of bed while sleeping?
3. Do you experience sudden muscle jerks as you fall asleep?
4. Do you talk or shout loudly during sleep?
5. Do you feel excessively tired during the day?
6. Do you have an irresistible urge to move your legs at night?
7. Do you wake up more than 3 times per night?
8. Have you injured yourself or a partner during sleep?
9. Are your dreams often frightening or intense?
10. Do you experience "Sleep Paralysis" frequently?

Section 2: Olfactory Function (10 Questions)

Assessing for Hyposmia (loss of smell), a key prodromal marker.

1. Has your general sense of smell decreased recently?
2. Is it hard to smell strong scents like coffee?
3. Does food taste "bland" compared to last year?
4. Can you smell a gas leak or smoke easily?
5. Has your ability to smell flowers diminished?
6. Is it hard to distinguish between different scents?
7. Has this change occurred in the last 2 years?
8. Do you need more salt/spice to enjoy food?
9. Is the smell of soap or detergent less sharp?
10. Do you miss smells that others mention?
